What is a drone inspection?

A drone inspection is a faster, safer, and less invasive form of inspection. Drone photography is more accurate that manual measurement or assessment. AI-assisted software can automatically locate damage that may be hard to locate or inaccessible on foot.

Why use a drone for insurance claims?

Drones can access locations that may be inaccessible otherwise. They lower the risk of further damage to the property.
